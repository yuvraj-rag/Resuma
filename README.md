# Resuma
A Resume analyzer powered by AI for analyzing and enhancing your resume and boosting ATS scores
=======
A Resume analyzer powered by AI for analyzing and enhancing your resume and boosting ATS scores.

Analyze your resume in just 2 steps : 

1. Upload your resume
2. Input desired job details and click analyze

Thats how easy it is!

Features : 
- Job specific resume feedback
- Suggestions on improving resume
- ATS scores and insights
- Quick and easy to use

Why Resuma?
- Helps your resume pass ATS filtering
- Highlights important skills and keywords
- Saves time and convinient to use
